# Credit Risk Module Foundation Complete (Lessons 1–4)

User has progressed through the full credit risk parameter set: PD (XGBoost vs scorecard, SHAP, SR 11-7), LGD (bimodal distribution, two-stage model, downturn LGD), EAD (CCF, panic draw, reference date problem), and Credit Scoring (alternative data, proxy variables, disparate impact). The MRM framework (SR 11-7 / PRA SS1/23) has been introduced and reinforced across all four lessons.

**Implications:** Market risk lessons (VaR, FRTB, algo trading, CCAR) can reference the MRM governance pattern without re-introducing it. The transition from credit risk to market risk introduces a fundamentally different model type — time-series, distributional, mark-to-market — which should be framed explicitly.
